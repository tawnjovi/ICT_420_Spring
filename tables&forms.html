A small summary of my favorite movies.
My Favorite Movies
Movie Name Summary
[Jojo Rabbit] A 10 year old boy learns to confront his blind nationalism with the aid of a Jewish girl during the mist of World War II.
[In the Heat of the Night] Police Detective Virgil Tibbs, after being wrongfully arrested, decides to help the small town of Sparta, Mississippi with a murder case.
[Top Gun] Maverick must compete with other fighter pilots at the Top Gun Naval Academy to be the best while also trying to win over the heart of his beautiful flight instructor.
[Forrest Gump] Forrest learns to overcome his own adversities to find great success and win over his love.
[Long Shot] Fred encounters an old crush and ends up working closely together with her- leading to the romance of his lifetime.
*Summaries may leave out key information from the movies.*
